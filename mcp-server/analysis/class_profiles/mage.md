## Mage Analysis Focus
- **Interrupts**: Counterspell has a long cooldown – was it used at the right moments?
- **Defensive CDs**: Ice Block, Mirror Image, Alter Time, Greater Invisibility usage.
- **Combustion/Icy Veins/Arcane Surge**: Major CD alignment with burst windows.
- **Remove Curse**: Was decursing needed and performed?
- **Spellsteal**: Were there beneficial buffs on enemies that could be stolen?
- **Time Warp/Bloodlust**: Was it used at the optimal moment?
- **Movement**: Shimmer/Blink usage for mechanics.
