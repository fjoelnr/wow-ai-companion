## Priest Analysis Focus
- **Dispels**: Mass Dispel and Purify/Purify Disease usage – critical in many encounters.
- **Defensives**: Fade, Desperate Prayer, Dispersion (Shadow), Pain Suppression/Guardian Spirit.
- **Power Infusion**: Was it used on the right target at the right time?
- **Interrupts**: Silence (Shadow) or Holy Word: Chastise (Holy) usage.
- **Healing CDs**: Divine Hymn, Salvation, Spirit Shell – proper timing with damage events.
- **Shadow Word: Death**: Finish/execute usage and self-damage management.
- **Leap of Faith**: Lifegrip usage to save others from mechanics.
