## Hunter Analysis Focus
- **Interrupts**: Counter Shot / Muzzle – hunters are top interrupters with short CDs.
- **Defensives**: Exhilaration, Aspect of the Turtle, Survival of the Fittest.
- **Misdirection**: Was Misdirect used to support the tank on pull?
- **Trueshot/Coordinated Assault/Fury of the Eagle**: Major CD alignment.
- **Pet Management**: Pet death = lost DPS. Was pet health maintained?
- **Binding Shot / Tar Trap**: CC utility usage for add control.
- **Tranquilizing Shot**: Enrage dispel on enemies when applicable.
